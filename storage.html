Service Details

STORAGE

Warehousing is a value added service offered by LOGISTIC SOLUTION to help simply your flow of goods and ensure your transportation costs are as low as possible. We have over 95,000 square feet of temperature regulated dry storage and 14 acres of yard space at our disposal. This means we can easily hold product until you need it, consolidate LTL shipments, cross-dock to maximize assets, and even palletize and ticket loose freight. We can also safely store trailers, containers, and equipment for efficient and timely processing. Our facilities are located in centralized regions throughout Canada and the I-5 corridor where they are needed most. Whether you need a little overflow space, access to a facility in a different region, or temporary storage to consolidate a load, our warehousing services are the perfect fit for your supply-chain needs.
